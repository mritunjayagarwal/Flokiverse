Flokiverse is a Metaverse based on the Vikings Era, where King Floki leads his armies to conquer the world and in pursuit of Valhalla. In flokiverse users will be able to build houses, craft weapons, build ships and invade land. Flokiverse will be set in islands and all islands will be connected by sea, every island will be owned, and defending that Island will gain rewards and conquering and island will gain 2x the reward in Flokiverse Currency.

What is FlokiVerse?

Flokiverse is a Metaverse and Valhalla city is it’s capital. Valhalla City will have its beginning starting with the Genesis district – the most prestigious district in the whole city, situated by the sea coast. The next districts of Flokicity will represent native platforms and products of the Flokiverse token ecosystem: Flokarium (nautic partly immersed under water district), Flokiverse NFT district and others. Each, one of a kind. Flokiverse play to earn scholarships, online events and meetups, virtual 3D galleries with art created by users, property/pet/accessories all will be tradeable, you can earn NFTs by undertaking different challenges – that is what will take place in Flokiverse and as our virtual world will be a completely immersive world built on Unity3D and Unreal Engine. The game will be playable on Oculus Quest 2, PC and MAC using the native flokiverse token.
